Our Story

We officially met as adults over Bumble. This meeting was fate, as we grew up in the same area, attended the same schools, and shared many mutual friends. Although our orbits took a while to cross, once they did, we quickly realized we were each other's favorite constellations.

From fated paths crossing due to shared beginnings to the final inevitable official collision, our story has always been written in the starlight. We can't wait to celebrate our stellar merger with you.
